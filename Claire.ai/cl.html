The Overview

Claire.ai is a network-oriented task collaboration platform turning
complex spoken and typed conversations around by creating to-
do lists and tasks. Claire.ai plays a role of an empathetic manager
of your projects and teams, helping you derive essential metrics
from insightful data taken from understanding how you
communicate. Through Natural Language Process (NLP),
Claire.ai is able to break down conversations to understand the
context, identify the intention and enable users to take the
appropriate action all on the fly.
